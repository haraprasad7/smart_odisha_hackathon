What Data is collected during registration ?

The data collected during regisrtaion will be as follows :

  • Personal identification details
  • Land and crop details
  • All documents related to crop insurance
  • Investment portfolio
  • Bank details (KISAN BANK)

How the data helps ?

The data collected will processed and the following information will be generated:

  • GROUPING OF FARMERS BASED ON - land property , crop type , area proximity
  • Investment Strata
  • Expected Premium and Insurance amount calculation
