WELCOME FARMERS

This portal help farmers know about their profile details and complete detailed plans of the scheme .

ABOUT THE SCHEME

This where the pruduct after the harvest goes. This will be helpful in the following ways:

  • Generation of yield data of farmers.
  • Automation of all transactions in farmer's KISAN BANK account
  • As no cash is involved in any transaction this protects farmers from being biased by merchants by selling their yields at cheaper rate.
  • The profit of yields will be distributed among farmers and investers according to their share.

SECURITY POLICY

The resources the farmer needs for the farming process are essential and hence must be verified source.

  • The farmers can only buy from verified sellers.
  • Protection of farmer from adultered seeds and pesticides through proper verification.
  • Each farmer recieves a package resources based on maximum yield data.
  • Automated deduction and addition of money in farmers account.
