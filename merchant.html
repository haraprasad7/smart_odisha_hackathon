BUSINESS PORTFOLIO

This portal gives the idea about the process of transaction of harvested crop between farmer to seller .

MERCHANTS

Relocation of harvsted crops . This will be helpful in the following ways:

  • Generates yield data while marketing the harvested crops
  • Help creates statices of the past 7-10 yrs of production.
  • Helps estimate the annual profits generated at a perticular region.

SELLERS

The portal help verify sellers for the marketing of scientifically tested fertilizers and pestisides.

  • The farmers can only buy from verified sellers with government certified licance.
  • The portal checks the marketing of cheap quality fertilizers and pesticides.
  • The money is automatically deducted from KISAN BANK account of the farmer on every purchase.
