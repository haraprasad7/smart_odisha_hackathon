GOVERNMENT OF ODISHA WELCOMES ALL INVESTORS

The portal helps investors , invest in agricultural sector with expectance of high annul returns.

ABOUT THE SCHEME :-

The portal provides a platform for investers to invest in agro-sector and gain annual returns.

  • This scheme is sighted on taking the orthodox loan system out of the senario.
  • This scheme will help in the developement of agricultural sector in future.
  • The investers will get seasonal returns according to their investment share.
  • The economy status of the state will rise drastically.

TYPES OF INVESTMENT SCHEMES:-

The investment strata is divided into two category:-

  • 1> INTRABASED INVESTMENT:-
  • In this type , investers from other work area invest in agricultural sector.
  • The referance of investment can be of thr following:-
  • i> Statics of past years yield of a farmer or a farmer group.
  • ii> Based on the weather statics of that region.
  • iii>Based on the type of crop the farmer is cultivating.

  • 2> INTERBASED INVESTMENT:-
  • In this type , one farmer can invest on other farmer.
  • F2F investing can be done as follows:-.
  • i> By sharing equity in land.
  • ii> By holding share on fertilizers and farming machinary.
  • iii> By holding equity on foundation seeds.
